Title: Matchy Grid

Tagline: Match Ideas, Spark Futures.

Username: happybee110

Project Description: Matchy Grid is a playful, single‑page web app designed to help beginners, students, and aspiring entrepreneurs discover business ideas and take their first steps toward building them.

Users select their interests, explore a soft aesthetic colorful grid of curated ideas, and receive suggestive pathways — simple, actionable starter steps for each idea. They can save favorites, experiment with filters or a “Surprise Me” option to spark creativity. A "Confused" option is also available for helping people guide through their intrests and sort out their wants and suggesting ideas based on the provided answers.

With its lightweight design Matchy Grid makes entrepreneurship accessible, engaging, and confidence‑boosting easily and quickly.
